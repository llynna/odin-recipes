Broccoli Salad

Description

A favorite at all our family gatherings. Left overs are just as good as the day it was made.

Ingredients

  • 1 head of broccoli chopped
  • 1 head of cauliflower chopped
  • 1 package of bacon cooked and crumbled
  • 1/2 red onion diced
  • 3/4 cup sliced almonds
  • 3/4 cup light mayo
  • 2 tablespoons red wine vinegar
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt

Steps

  1. Mix mayo, vinegar and salt
  2. In seperate dish, add broccoli, cauliflower, bacon, onion and almonds
  3. Combine the mayo dressing with the broccoli mix
  4. Add craisins, cubed chedder cheese or sunflower seeds if desired
