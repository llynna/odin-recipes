Caprese Salad

Description

This is a quick and easy salad that does not disappoint! Red wine vinegar can be substituted for the balsamic glaze if prefered. Friends and family will love this light flavorful dish.

Ingredients

  • 16oz cherry size mozerella balls
  • 1/4 cup fresh chopped basil
  • 1 diced avocado
  • 2 cups cherry tomatos
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • 1/8 cup balsamic glaze
  • garlic and onion powder to taste

Steps

  1. Mix olive oil, glaze, and seasonings
  2. In seperate dish, mix mozerella, basil, avocado and tomatos
  3. Right before serving, add the oil mixture to the mozerella mixture
