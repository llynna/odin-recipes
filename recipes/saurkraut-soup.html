Saurkraut Soup

Description

Great soup for all seasons! Family will love the flavor of this dish. Easy to throw in the crockpot and ready when you are home from work.

Ingredients

  • 1lb polish sausage cut into pieces
  • 5 medium potatos peeled and cubed
  • 2 large onions chopped
  • 2 large carrots sliced
  • 42oz can of chicken broth
  • 32oz bag of saurkraut
  • 6oz can of tomato paste

Steps

  1. Add ingredients to crock pot
  2. Cook on low for 8 hours
  3. Serve with rye bread
